# 🫁 Lungs Guard AI

## AI-Powered Lung Health Risk Assessment and Monitoring Platform

Lungs Guard AI is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to assist in the early assessment of lung-related health risks through AI-driven analysis, symptom evaluation, medical imaging support, and real-time monitoring capabilities.

The platform combines predictive analytics, health data processing, symptom assessment, and explainable AI techniques to provide meaningful insights that can support proactive healthcare decision-making.

---

## Overview

Respiratory diseases remain one of the leading causes of global health challenges. Early identification of symptoms and risk factors can significantly improve healthcare outcomes.

Lungs Guard AI leverages Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to analyze health-related information, evaluate potential lung disease risks, monitor patient conditions, and generate actionable insights through an intuitive healthcare dashboard.

## Core Modules

### Risk Engine

Performs intelligent lung health risk calculations using machine learning models and health indicators.

### Symptom Service

Analyzes patient-reported symptoms to identify potential respiratory concerns.

### X-Ray Service

Processes and evaluates medical imaging data to support diagnostic assessment.

### Cough Service

Extracts audio features and analyzes cough patterns for respiratory risk evaluation.

### Oxygen Monitor

Tracks oxygen saturation levels and identifies abnormal conditions in real time.

### Alert System

Provides immediate notifications when critical health thresholds are detected.

### Explainability Module

Generates transparent explanations behind AI-generated predictions and recommendations.
